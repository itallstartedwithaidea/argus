Submit a Profile or Post

Paste any social media profile URL or post/thread URL. Works for profiles, posts with suspicious engagement, bot comment threads, and fake like clusters.

⚖️ ALGORITHMIC ANALYSIS, NOT A VERDICT
ARGUS produces algorithmic opinion based on publicly available signals. It does not make legal, factual, or identity determinations. All results are subject to human review. Profile owners can dispute at any time.
Supported: LinkedIn, Reddit, Instagram, X (Twitter), Facebook, YouTube, Quora, TikTok, Pinterest
Can't share a public URL? Check this to paste content directly — works for private accounts, DMs, stories, deleted posts, or anything you can see but ARGUS can't scrape.
Copy-paste as much context as you can. More data = better analysis. Include comments, engagement numbers, timestamps, etc.
Screenshots give ARGUS visual context — profile pics, post layouts, comment sections, engagement metrics. Max 5 files, 10MB each.
Describe what looks suspicious. Bot patterns, fake engagement, AI-generated content, coordinated behavior, etc.
